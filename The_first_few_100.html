Gameplay!

In The Few 100 you begin as a tribe of 10 people in the stone age. You are represented by a stickman carying spear.
The stickman is standing on a tile. All around him are other tiles with different icons, there cound be trees, deer, mountains etc..
At the begining of your turn you can stay on, or move onto another tile. You will then recieve 3 random options to chose from.
For example: if you move to a forest tile, you can:

  1. Investigate a strange buzzing sound,
  2. Colect branches,
  3. Pick fruit from trees,

When you chose one of options you will get different results. For the buzzing sound you might discover honey -which will give you food
(an essential reource) or recieve damage to your tribe from being stung by bees, or both.
over the turns you will discover new technologies. An example is making tents.
If you have colected wood and then hunt animals you will be able to join hides with branches to make a tent.
each technology will give you new advantages: the tent will keep you warm at night, meaning you will need less food to fuel your tribe
